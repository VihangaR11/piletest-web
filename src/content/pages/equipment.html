From Field Measurement to Engineering Interpretation

Each system is selected around the test objective, anticipated capacity, site access and governing method. Measurements are captured in the field, checked for quality and interpreted by the technical team.

01 / Dynamic testing

Pile Driving Analyzer (PDA-8G)

A Pile Dynamics Inc. system that records strain and acceleration during impact. The measurements support field assessment of pile capacity, integrity, driving stress and hammer performance, followed by CAPWAP signal matching where required.

Method referenceASTM D4945
Listed range250-75,000 kN
Impact hammersUp to 80 tonnes
Post-processingCAPWAP

  • Shaft and toe resistance
  • Driving stresses
  • Hammer performance
