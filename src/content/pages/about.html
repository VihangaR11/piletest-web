Company overview

Quality Assurance for Deep Foundations

PileTeST Consultants (PVT) LTD is an independent testing and geotechnical engineering company established in Sri Lanka to provide reliable measurements for foundation and construction decisions.

The company supports consultants, contractors, developers and infrastructure teams through pile testing, geotechnical investigation, non-destructive testing, laboratory testing, foundation monitoring and specialist engineering services.

Field measurements are supported by experienced professionals, recognized methods, calibrated specialist systems and technical reporting prepared for practical project use.

Our journey

Growing from Specialist Testing to Integrated Engineering Support

The company has expanded its capability while keeping independent measurement and technical quality at the center of its work.

  1. 2014

    Company Established

    PileTeST Consultants was incorporated in Sri Lanka to provide specialist pile-testing and foundation quality-assurance services.

  2. Expansion

    Broader Testing Capability

    Services expanded into static and instrumented load testing, integrity testing, cross-hole sonic logging and construction monitoring.

  3. Integration

    Geotechnical & Laboratory Services

    Ground investigation, laboratory testing, non-destructive testing and engineering support were added to serve more complete project requirements.

  4. Through 2024

    48,693 Recorded Tests

    The published records include 2,634 PDA tests, 38,002 PIT tests and 8,057 CSL tests.
