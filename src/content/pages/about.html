Company profile

Independent Foundation Testing Expertise

PileTeST Consultants (PVT) LTD was incorporated in Sri Lanka to provide pile testing, geotechnical engineering and foundation-monitoring services through experienced professionals.

The company operates an ISO 9001:2015-certified quality management system accredited by SLAB and RvA Netherlands. Its technical capability includes equipment and systems developed for deep-foundation testing and construction monitoring.

Services have expanded from dynamic pile testing into integrity testing, static and instrumented load testing, geotechnical investigation, laboratory testing, vibration and noise monitoring, and structural movement monitoring.
