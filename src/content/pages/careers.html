03 / Graduate training

Graduate Expression of Interest

Graduate opportunities depend on active requirements. When a suitable placement exists, development may combine supervised field work, method learning and reporting discipline.

  1. 01

    Safety & Method Orientation

    Understand work controls, equipment hazards and the purpose of each measurement.

  2. 02

    Supervised Field Exposure

    Observe preparation, instrumentation, acquisition and test documentation.

  3. 03

    Data & Quality Review

    Learn traceability, measurement checks and the limits of engineering evidence.

  4. 04

    Technical Communication

    Support clear records and reporting under experienced professional review.

This pathway describes potential learning areas, not a guaranteed programme, placement duration or employment offer.
