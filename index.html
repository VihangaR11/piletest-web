Core Services

Comprehensive Foundation Testing Solutions

From dynamic testing to geotechnical investigations — covering the full spectrum of pile testing and foundation monitoring.

📡

Dynamic Pile Testing (PDA)

High strain testing using PDA®-8G. Evaluates bearing capacity, integrity and driving stresses. Range: 250 kN to 75,000 kN.

ASTM D4945 →
🔊

PIT Low Strain Testing

Quick integrity verification of all piles on site using pulse echo method. Detects cracks, voids and diameter reductions.

ASTM D5882 →
🔬

Cross-Hole Sonic Logging

Concrete quality verification in drilled shafts. 35+ years of standardized practice. ASTM and AFNOR compliant.

ASTM D6760 →
⚖️

Static Load Testing

Direct loading up to 40,000 kN using Kentledge and hydraulic jacks. The gold standard of pile performance assessment.

BS 8004 →
📳

Vibration Monitoring

Continuous x/y/z vibration and noise monitoring during construction to protect adjacent structures and communities.

DIN 4150 →
🌍

Geotechnical Investigation

SPT, SCPT, DCPT, inclinometers, piezometers, settlement monitoring, and comprehensive laboratory soil testing.

BS EN ISO 22476 →
