Testing Systems

Primary Equipment

PDA®-8G
Pile Driving Analyzer 8G
The most widely employed system for Dynamic Load Testing and Pile Driving Monitoring worldwide. Developed by Pile Dynamics Inc., USA. Features Smart Strain Gauges and Smart Accelerometers. ASTM D4945 compliant. Load capacity: 250 kN to 75,000 kN with hammers up to 80 Ton.
CAPABILITIES
Bearing Capacity Shaft Integrity Driving Stress CAPWAP® Analysis
PIT-QV
Pile Integrity Tester
Low-strain sonic echo/pulse echo testing. Evaluates concrete and timber foundations. Detects major cracks, voids and diameter reductions. ASTM D5882. Includes PIT-W® analysis software.
CHAMP
Cross-Hole Analyzer
Ultrasonic probes 40–60 kHz range. Cross-hole and single-hole sonic logging. Digital encoder for depth recording. Water-coupled acoustic measurements. ASTM D6760.
IMLT
Instrumented Load Test System
Displacement transducers, strain gauges, mechanical tell-tale extensometers, load cells and data loggers. 100 kN to 40,000 kN range.
VMS
Vibration Monitoring System
Continuous X/Y/Z vibration monitoring. Frequency (Hz) and Peak (mm/s) recording. Real-time digital signal processing. DIN 4150 compliant.
INC
Inclinometers & Piezometers
Lateral movement detection for retaining structures. Borehole installation for permanent monitoring. Ground water pressure measurement.
Analysis Software

Software Suite

CAPWAP®
Case Pile Wave Analysis Program. Estimates total bearing capacity, resistance distribution along shaft and at toe. Simulates static load test.
GRLWEAP
Wave equation analysis program for pile driving prediction. Optimizes hammer selection, driving criteria and pile installation.
PIT-W®
Pile Integrity Test analysis software. Graphical representation of velocity signals. Time domain and frequency domain analysis.
iCAP
Real-time data collection and analysis for PDA testing. Integrated capacity assessment during pile driving operations.
