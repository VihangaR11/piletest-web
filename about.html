Our Story

Who We Are

PileTeST Consultants (PVT) LTD was incorporated in Sri Lanka specifically to provide pile testing and geotechnical engineering services with high-level competent professionals. The company was certified for ISO 9001:2015 which was accredited by SLAB and RvA Netherlands.

We enjoy the backing of Pile Dynamics Inc., USA — the pioneer and manufacturer of the Pile Driving Analyzer®. The company provides expert geotechnical solutions through eminent professionals from Sri Lankan universities and industry.

Our scope of services has expanded over the years to meet the ever-evolving needs of our clients: geotechnical designs, settlement monitoring, lateral displacement monitoring using inclinometers, testing instrumented piles, vibration monitoring, crack monitoring and field and laboratory geotechnical testing.
